Surgical Dentistry

Extraction, implantation, and plastic surgery — precise, safe, and pain-free.

We perform all types of oral surgeries: from tooth extractions and soft tissue corrections to full All-on-4 / All-on-6 implantation. All procedures are done with comfort and precision.

What we do:

  • Tooth extraction

    Simple and complex cases, including wisdom teeth removal.

  • Frenectomy and soft tissue correction

    Improving speech, bite alignment, or preparation for dentures.

  • Bone grafting and sinus lifting

    Rebuilding bone volume for successful implant placement.

  • All-on-4 / All-on-6 implantation

    Complete restoration with temporary prosthesis within 2–5 days.

Why choose us:

  • Accurate 3D diagnostics and digital planning
  • Modern anesthesia — no pain during procedures
  • Premium quality implants from trusted manufacturers
  • Continuous support through all treatment stages

Frequently Asked Questions

Is tooth extraction painful?

No. We use modern anesthesia to ensure the procedure is completely pain-free.

How much does All-on-4 implantation cost?

It depends on the treatment plan. We provide transparent pricing and payment options.

Can I get implants if I have low bone volume?

Yes. We perform bone grafting and sinus lifting to restore bone and make implant placement possible.

How long does recovery take after sinus lifting?

Usually between 5 and 7 days. Our team will guide you throughout the recovery process.
