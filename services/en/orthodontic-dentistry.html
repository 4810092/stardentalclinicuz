Orthodontic Dentistry

Star Dental Clinic in Tashkent offers modern solutions for straightening teeth: braces, aligners and retainers. Suitable for both adults and children.

Our Services

  • Orthodontic consultation: digital diagnostics, 3D planning and treatment roadmap.
  • Braces:
    • Metal — durable and reliable.
    • Ceramic and sapphire — discreet yet effective.
    • Self-ligating — modern and comfortable with fewer visits.
  • Aligners: transparent removable trays. Aesthetic and convenient.
  • Children’s treatment: trainers, aligners, orthotropic methods. The earlier — the better.
  • Retention: preserve the results with custom retainers and guidance.

Frequently Asked Questions

At what age can braces be applied?

Usually from 11–13 years, after permanent teeth have erupted.

How long does orthodontic treatment take?

Typically between 12 to 24 months, depending on the complexity.

Which is better: aligners or braces?

Aligners are great for mild to moderate cases. Braces are more effective for complex misalignments.
