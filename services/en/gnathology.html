Gnathology

If you suffer from jaw pain, clicking sounds, teeth grinding, or headaches — we focus on diagnosing and resolving the root cause.

We provide precise diagnostics, target treatment for TMJ dysfunction, bruxism, and bite issues, and create custom night guards to protect your teeth and relieve tension.

Gnathology is for you if:

  • You experience jaw, facial, neck, or head pain
  • You wake up with clenched teeth
  • You notice popping or clicking in your jaw joint
  • You’ve had chronic discomfort with no clear cause

We collaborate with orthodontists, prosthodontists, and general dentists to restore balance — making it easier to eat, speak, and live comfortably.
