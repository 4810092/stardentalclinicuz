Prosthetic Dentistry

Your smile — full of confidence again.

Even one missing tooth can impact not just your appearance but the overall health of your jaw system. We offer comprehensive and modern dental restoration solutions — reliable, natural-looking, and customized to your anatomy.

Our Services

  • Fixed prosthetics — crowns and bridges made from ceramic, zirconia, or metal-ceramic. Natural, aesthetic, and long-lasting.
  • Veneers and overlays — ideal for correcting shape, color, and minor imperfections.
  • Removable dentures — modern designs that are comfortable to wear and easy to maintain.
  • Implant-supported prosthetics — restore not only aesthetics but full chewing function.
  • Digital prosthetics (CAD/CAM) — precise restorations without impressions and long wait times.
  • Repair and adjustments — we resolve any discomfort or issues with your prosthetics quickly.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a dental crown cost?

The cost depends on the material — ceramic, zirconia, or metal-ceramic. Ask our doctor during consultation.

Can veneers be placed without shaving teeth?

Yes, in some cases ultra-thin veneers can be placed without preparation. The dentist will advise.

How to care for removable dentures?

Rinse them daily under running water and use special cleaning tablets or solutions.
